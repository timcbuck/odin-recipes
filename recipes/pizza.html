Pizza

Description

No pizza oven! No problem! Make your homemade pizza on the grill for that classic smoky, wood fire oven flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 package active dry yeast.
  • 1 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Make dough.
  3. Mix in flour.
  4. Add sauce
  5. Bake in oven.
